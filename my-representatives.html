Federal Elected Officials

Georgia is represented in Congress by 2 U.S. Senators and 14 U.S. Representatives. Senators serve six-year terms and represent the entire state. Representatives serve two-year terms and represent individual congressional districts.

Find Your U.S. Legislator

Select your State and Chamber (House or Senate). A list of your Representatives or Senators will appear in the drop-down list. Many counties are served by multiple districts, and some districts serve multiple counties.

If you are unsure which district you're in, you can use the GA Secretary of State My Voter Page tool. Enter your information and scroll to the bottom of the page to see your districts (both at the federal and state level). Then, you can identify which member and/or candidate is in your district.
